About Us

Our founder and Clinic Director, Dian, established Immaculate Health to provide holistic care underpinned by the highest ethical and professional standards.
Meet Dian Wirawan, BA(Hons), MPsychOrg (UQ)
Principal Psychologist & Clinic Director With over 15 years of experience, Dian Wirawan is a compassionate psychologist based in Brisbane, specialising in supporting children with behavioural and emotional challenges. She also works extensively with CALD communities, individuals with special needs, and clients facing complex psychological challenges, delivering evidence-based, empathetic care. Dian's therapeutic approaches include: CBT, TF-CBT, DBT, ACT, Mindfulness, Motivational Interviewing, Positive Psychology, and parenting interventions such as Triple-P, 123 Magic Parenting, and Nurtured Heart.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a referral to book an appointment?
No referral is needed to book an appointment.
Do you offer online therapy sessions?
Yes, online therapy sessions via telehealth are available upon agreement.
How long is each session?
Each session lasts for about 50 minutes.
How many sessions will I need?
The number of sessions is determined based on your unique needs, with guidance from your therapist.
Where are these sessions held?
In-person counselling sessions occur at our clinic in Sunnybank, QLD.
Can I access Telehealth counselling?
Yes, with a prior agreement with your clinician.
How often do the sessions occur?
Counselling sessions usually occur every fortnight, although weekly sessions may be arranged based on clinical considerations.
Do you accept Medicare rebates or private health insurance?
Yes, we accept both. Please ensure you have the necessary referrals.
What are the costs of therapy sessions?
Session fees vary. Please refer to the Pricing & Insurance section for detailed information.
Are my therapy sessions confidential? Absolutely. Your privacy is our utmost priority.
What should I expect during my first session?
Your initial session involves a comprehensive discussion of your concerns and an introduction to our approach.
What is your Cancellation Policy?
When you book an appointment, a specific time slot is reserved for you. Please notify us at least 48 hours in advance if you need to cancel. A cancellation fee of $100 applies for notifications given less than 48 hours in advance. For cancellations/rescheduling with less than 24 hours’ notice, a fee of $95 will be charged, and missed sessions are charged at the full session fee. These fees do not attract a Medicare rebate. Three repeat cancellations or no-shows may result in a review of your treatment plan.
